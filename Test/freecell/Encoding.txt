When supermove/Stack move
Shows the bottom card of the stack (The card most in front of the stack) and then shows the number of cards in the stack as shown below:
Move 3 cards and the bottom card is 2 of hearts: 2h#3 (2h is the bottom card and 3 is the number of cards, including the bottom card)

Encoded string for step examples,
Move the 3 of hearts card from column 3 to column 7: 3h_2_~6~
Move 2 cards stack with the bottom card being the 7 of diamonds card from column 4 to column 8: 7d#2_3_~7~
Move K of clubs from column 5 to Frecell/Reserve: kc_4_R
Move Q of spades from column 1 to Foundation: qs_0_F

The entire solution will be a one-line string, and each step separated with the '|' sign
Example,
Step 1: Move the 3 of hearts card from column 3 to column 7
Step 2: Move 2 card stacks with the bottom card being the 7 of diamonds card from column 4 to column 8

String: 3h_2_~6~|7d#2_3_~7~
