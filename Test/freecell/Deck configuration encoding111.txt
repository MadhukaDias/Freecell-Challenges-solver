For Freecells and Foundation

[]  [H ] [C ] [D ] [S ]

For freecells there are 4 slots and same for foundation. initally all of them are free.

Empty slot will indicate with two 00s.

First 8 0s' for indicate blank freecells. Couple of 0s' indicates one blank slot in freecells. Same for foundation.
